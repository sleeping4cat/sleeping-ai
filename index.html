Sleeping AI

Sleeping AI—we’re here to disrupt, change, and reinvent SOTA.

Who We Are

We are a frontier AI research lab dedicated to expanding human perspectives on artificial intelligence and exploring its deep integration into society to accelerate our path toward a Tier 1 civilization.

Our focus is on tackling overlooked challenges and underexplored subfields where innovation is urgently needed. Unlike OpenAI or DeepMind, we refuse to constrain ourselves to a single architecture or predefined path. Instead, we operate as a collective of research hackers—pushing boundaries, questioning norms, and pursuing breakthroughs that others ignore.

Scalability, real-world impact, and the long-term future are at the core of everything we do.

Our Mission

In a world where OpenAI, Safe Superintelligence, and DeepMind dominate the AI landscape, we are not in a race to define or claim AGI. We make no grand promises of achieving it first—because that is not our mission.

We exist for a far simpler yet more critical purpose: AI research needs a lab that explores the vast, overlooked subfields of artificial intelligence, commits to open-source principles, and publishes freely for the advancement of humanity. That is why, while we call ourselves a lab, we are, in reality, a collective—a group of ambitious, relentless individuals who push the boundaries of research, regardless of the controversy or resistance we may face.

In the future, we aim to become investors, spinning off our research into independent startups that will challenge the dominance of larger labs and corporations. Yet, Sleeping AI itself will remain untethered—driven not by profit, but by the pursuit of knowledge and the relentless push for AI that serves humanity.
