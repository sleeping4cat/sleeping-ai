About Sleeping AI

We're a team of researchers from across the world with different backgrounds, ranging from Ethical Hacking to Quantum Computing. Our teammates come from highly respected institutions and labs to work on a singular objective of researching and finding bleeding-edge solutions in various fields. Though we mainly focus on Artificial Intelligence and Quantum Computing.

Being a small team, we focus our resources on questions that really matter. Though we push boundaries in research, we make sure our projects align with our ethical guidelines, which, in turn, slows the pace of publishing. This can be considered a necessary evil.

While we're a research lab primarily, some of our projects are transitioned to startups as well. For more information, please email: sleeping4cat@outlook.com.
